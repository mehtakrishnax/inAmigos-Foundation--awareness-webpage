Who We Are

A Youth-Led Paradigm in Social Evolution

Founded on September 23, 2020, in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, the InAmigos Foundation arose from a simple realization: youth desire to make a difference, but lack highly organized platforms to execute their drive. Today, we stand as one of India's fastest-growing youth-led Section 8 non-profits, merging digital innovation, educational support, and rapid-response disaster relief.

01
Youth Empowerment

We run a highly successful skill development and internship platform (Project Vikas) equipping over 8,000+ interns with real-world skills while running grassroot campaigns.

02
Holistic Intervention

We don't focus on a single cause. We believe that true community upliftment requires simultaneous attention to education, hunger, female hygiene, environmental balance, and animal safety.

03
Radical Transparency

As a government-registered entity with 80G and 12A approvals, every donation and expense is logged, audited, and strictly optimized to ensure maximum grassroots reach.

Our Impact Pillars

Ongoing Campaigns & Projects

We channel our efforts through six dedicated projects, each focused on solving a specific, critical element of societal well-being.

Project SEVA

Feeding Hope, Clothing Dignity

Project SEVA is our flagship nutrition and emergency aid campaign. We organize daily feeding drives, supply monthly ration kits to marginalized daily-wage families, and distribute warm blankets and clean clothing during winters.

Impact: 50,000+ meals and 5,000+ clothing kits distributed.
Project BACHPANSHALA

Digital Literacy & Life Skills

Bachpanshala aims to bridge the digital and educational divide. We establish local weekend learning centers, donate books, provide computers, and deliver digital skills, basic English communication, and sanitation education to street children.

Impact: 12,000+ children taught across 15 learning cells.
Project UDAAN

Women's Hygiene & Empowerment

Project UDAAN focuses on removing menstrual taboos and providing financial independence. We conduct sanitary pad distribution camps, raise reproductive hygiene awareness, and run free sewing and craft vocational workshops.

Impact: 8,000+ sanitary pads distributed & 450+ women trained.
🐾
Project JEEV

Stray Animal Rescue & Care

Project JEEV champions animal welfare. Our volunteers run daily feeding routes for stray dogs and cattle, collaborate with veterinarians to provide prompt rescue treatments, and install reflective collars to prevent road accidents.

Impact: 3,500+ animals fed daily and 600+ rescued from injuries.
🌱
Project PRAKRITI

Environmental Restoration

Prakriti works toward a green, clean future. We organize massive plantation drives in urban areas, run cleanup campaigns in public parks and riversides, and conduct eco-conservation seminars to eliminate single-use plastic.

Impact: 15,000+ saplings planted with a 90% survival rate.
💼
Project VIKAS

Youth Internships & Mentorship

Project VIKAS operates our massive nationwide internship system. We select students from all over India, providing them hands-on exposure in digital marketing, social work, HR, graphic design, and leadership development.

Impact: 8,000+ students certified and mentored.
